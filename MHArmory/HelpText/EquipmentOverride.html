Equipment override

Overview

Overriding equipment is a way for you to tell Armory to exclude some armor pieces and charms in your research for sets that match your desired skills.

By default, if you do not activate equipment override, Armory considers you have all equipments, based on the events selection you made. If you activate equipment override on the main window, then only checked equipment in the equipment override window will be taken into account in search results. Note that events are still taken into account for exclusion.

If you activate equipment override and check all equipments, this will produce the same result as with the feature disabled.
